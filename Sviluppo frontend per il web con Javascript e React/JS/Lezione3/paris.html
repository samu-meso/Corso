Paris

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France. It is the political, economic, and cultural center of France. The city is located in northern central France, in the Île-de-France region, at the heart of a large metropolitan area. Paris has been one of the world's major centers of art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture for over two centuries. It is home to many world-famous landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.
